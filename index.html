Shown in local time

Historical METAR data from the Iowa Environmental Mesonet ASOS archive. Observations are decoded for flight category, ceiling, visibility, wind, and weather phenomena. To eliminate bias from SPECI reporting, analyses collapse observations into one slot per UTC hour per day, then forward/backward-fill gaps — so each clock-hour carries equal weight regardless of how many reports were issued. The Computed Average Day synthesizes a representative METAR by aggregating all observations matching that calendar date across the selected time period.
